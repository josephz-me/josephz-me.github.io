Joseph is an interface designer system-level interaction patterns and multimodal experiences. Currently, he is an undergraduate at the Carnegie Mellon School of Design and Human-Computer Interaction Institute. He believes in developing a more humane way for people to engage with tech and screen-based interfaces.

Previously, he was an Interaction Design Intern at Apple (S’20) building conversational voice patterns and a UX Design Intern at Elevation Church (S'19). For the next few months, he will be designing intent-based interfaces with Brain Technologies (F’20).
